Konkani Annual Picnic 2024

Date: September 15, 2024

Location: Melbourne, Australia

A fun-filled day of outdoor activities and relaxation at the Konkani Annual Picnic. The event was marked by various games, live music, and an assortment of delicious Konkani dishes. Families and friends enjoyed a day of bonding, celebrating their cultural heritage, and creating lasting memories. A renowned Konkani chef was a special guest at the picnic, providing a culinary experience that highlighted traditional Konkani flavors.
