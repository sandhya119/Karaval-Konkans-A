Heritage Day 2024

Date: June 20, 2024

Location: Brisbane, Australia

Heritage Day was an event dedicated to honoring the rich history and traditions of the Konkani community. The event featured storytelling, traditional attire, cultural performances, and historical exhibits. Attendees immersed themselves in the cultural heritage of the Konkani people, with a special guest appearance by a prominent cultural historian, who shared insights into the origins and evolution of Konkani culture and heritage.These events aimed to celebrate and preserve Konkani culture while bringing the community together through memorable experiences.

Special guest: A prominent cultural historian
