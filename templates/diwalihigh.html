Diwali Celebration 2024

Date: November 10, 2024

Location: Sydney, Australia

A grand celebration of the Festival of Lights, Diwali. The event featured traditional Konkani performances, delicious homemade food, and cultural showcases. Families and friends gathered to celebrate the joyous occasion with vibrant decorations, music, dance, and a special guest performance by a classical dancer. This celebration aimed to bring the community together while showcasing the rich traditions of the Konkani culture.

Special guest: A renowned classical dancer
